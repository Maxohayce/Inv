Why invest with Olive ?

Olive health foundation is a platform that helps potential transplant recipients find matching donors by linking the database of healthcare facilities worldwide and also a platform that connects hospitals and healthcare centers with various Medtech providers worlwide. It is a global database, Blockchain-based system created and run using Hyperledger and secured with encrypted tools. Olive Increases the chance to find rapidly a proper match while keeping your data safe and protected. Privacy and confidentiality are preserved thanks to encryption tools and certificates are issued by authorized healthcare entities.

Why invest in health with Olive?
Olive HEALTH offers the opportunity to extend paired exchange for all incompatible donor-recipient pairs and provider -health center pairs in a secure and rapid way. We earn from fees paid by partners and healthcare facilities for remaining a part of the LEM ecosystem. Other revenue sources include investments by partners and venture capitalists, as well as commissions paid by recipients after finding a matching donor.
